FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

  1. When should I use a particular Articulating Manipulator?

    The parallel jaw should be used when force is required.The v-jaw has a better range of motion so it is capable of grasping bigger objects. The sampler takes dirt samples. The trident grasps round objects, and the cutter cuts.

  2. How much grip strength does the Manipulator have?

    The Articulating Manipulator closes with a maximum of 25 PSI.
