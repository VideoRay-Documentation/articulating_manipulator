Articulating Manipulator Functions

The operator can use the Articulating Manipulator to grasp,scoop up,cut,and retrieve submerged objects. The Articulating Manipulator's five jaws allow it to retrieve items in confined or hazardous locations. The jaws can be removed and attached as needed. The Articulating Manipulator has pincer points and a blade, use caution when interacting closely with these jaws.
