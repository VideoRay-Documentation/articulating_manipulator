Articulating Manipulator Overview

What is the Articulating Manipulator?

The VideoRay Articulating Manipulator Arms are a collection of grasping accessories that give the user a wide range of options when using the ROV to interact with the environment.

What is the Articulating Manipulator's purpose?

The Articulating Manipulator is designed to allow the user greater flexibility when using the ROV.The Articulating Manipulator Arms lift objects underwater by using the tether of the ROV.

How is the Articulating Manipulator operated?

The controller operates the Articulating Manipulator on a two button system. One button opens, and one button closes. The cutter however, has controls that are inverted.

What are some of the Articulating Manipulator's unique features?

The five different arms can perform a variety of tasks such as untangling tether snags,cutting rope, collecting samples and recovering objects of up to 45 kg.
