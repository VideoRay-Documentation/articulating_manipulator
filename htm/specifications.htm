The specifications provide more in depth product detail about the individual jaws. On this page you can see the Articulating Manipulator's weight in and out of water, how powerful each jaw is, and the pressure that is generated from each jaw's closing force. The maximum depth rating for the manipulator is 300 meters. The Articulating Manipulator runs off of 12 volts with a maximum of about 1 amp of current. This comes out to 12 watts of power consumption.
